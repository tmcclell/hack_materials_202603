Hack Kickoff

⏱ 20 minutes
🎯 Key Message
"Pick your challenge, form your team, and start building."

Kickoff

  • Use-case themes: what real projects can you Copilot-ify?
  • Team formation: 3–5 people, mix disciplines
  • Repo access: use your sandbox repo or create a new one
  • Fallback track for teams without use cases (3 guided specs — see hacking-guide.html)
  • Recommended workflow: Start with Plan Mode → let it propose steps → approve → switch to Agent/Autopilot
  • WiFi, power, bathroom locations
🎯 Key Message
"Quick progress share — what's working, what's stuck, what's surprising?"

Mid-Hack Check-In

▸ 2–3 min per team: what are you building? What's your status?
▸ Facilitators highlight interesting discoveries
▸ Unblock stuck teams with quick tips
"If you're ahead of schedule, try Autopilot for the remaining work."
