Show & Tell

⏱ 35 minutes
🎯 Key Message
"Show us what you built! Focus on the Copilot journey, not just the result."

Format

  • ~5 minutes per team

Demo Template

1
The Challenge — What were you trying to build?
2
The Copilot Features — Which modes/features did you use? (Agent, Plan, Autopilot, Chat, etc.)
3
Live Demo — Show it working!
4
Surprises — What surprised you? What worked better/worse than expected?

Tips for a Good Demo

💾 Have a backup plan (screenshots if live demo fails)
💡 Focus on the "aha moment"
🤝 Be honest about what didn't work
Audience: ask questions! This is a conversation, not a presentation.
